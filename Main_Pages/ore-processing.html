ORE PROCESSING

One of the main draws to Mekanism is its unique and scientific based approach to ore multiplying. In the base game, we have the fortune enchantment. With no enchantment on a pickaxe, you would get just 1 diamond from a diamond ore block. With fortune 3, that jumps up to an average of 2, with the maximum of 4. However it’s not guaranteed to get 4 or even 2 every time. With ore processing, not only are the conversion rates the same every time, but at the maximum tier, 3 raw ores will give you 10 ingots.

TIER ONE

The flowchart shown here is a bit of a scary image, but we will be going through it step by step, tier by tier. Tier one only involves a machine we already have: the enrichment chamber. As we know, we can place some materials in and get their enriched versions, but we can also place raw ore inside. Inserting 3 raw ore will give you 4 of its corresponding dusts. Those dusts can then be smelted into ingots, and thus, you have multiplied your ore by 1.3x. If you’re not interested in making many machines and processes in the chart, you can stop here. Having an extra ingot is already pretty good, but we can multiply that by 3.33x.
